## What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that visited websites send to the user's browser, where they are stored and then retransmitted to the same websites on subsequent visits.

## Cookies used by this website

### Technical cookies
This website may use technical session cookies and cookies for saving cookie consent preferences. These cookies do not require user consent as they are strictly necessary for the operation of the website.

### Third-party cookies

**MioDottore widget (Docplanner Italia S.r.l.)**
This website integrates a booking widget provided by MioDottore (Docplanner). This service may install its own cookies for the operation of the widget and for profiling purposes. For more information:
- [MioDottore Privacy Policy](https://www.miodottore.it/privacy-policy)
- [MioDottore Cookie Policy](https://www.miodottore.it/cookie-policy)

**iubenda (iubenda S.r.l.)**
This website uses iubenda Privacy Controls and Cookie Solution for cookie consent management. iubenda may install a technical cookie to store the user's consent preferences. For more information: [iubenda Privacy Policy](https://www.iubenda.com/privacy-policy/252372).

**GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc.)**
The hosting service may set technical cookies. For more information: [GitHub Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Services without cookies

**Cloudflare Web Analytics**
This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis. This service **does not use cookies**, does not collect personal data, and does not track users across different websites. It is mentioned here for transparency only. For more information: [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## How to manage cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through the banner displayed on your first visit, or at any time by clicking "Manage cookie preferences" in the website footer.

You can also disable cookies directly from your browser:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
